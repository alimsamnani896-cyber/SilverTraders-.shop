About Us

Silver Traders is a trusted retailer and wholesaler of high-quality steel products located in Keshod, Gujarat. We deal in branded steel materials like Tata Steel, Apollo Steel Pipes, Sanbhav Steel Pipes, Jindal JSW Steel, MS Pipes, TMT Bars, MS Channels and other steel hardware materials.

We provide best quality steel materials at the most affordable price compared to the market rate.

We serve both retail and wholesale customers, supplying premium steel products for construction, fabrication, and industrial projects. We DO NOT sell any PVC pipes - only genuine steel products.
